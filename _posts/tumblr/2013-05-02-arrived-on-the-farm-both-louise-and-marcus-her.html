Arrived on the farm. Both Louise and Marcus (her son), are fantastically nice. Mark is on Faray preparing for lambing season, so I have yet to meet him.

On the way up, I saw the local owl as well as some of of the teensy bunnies that the farm is “infested” with, as Louise called it.

She also mentioned that there is a ranger on the island who could lead me on walks, etc., which sounds like an awesome opportunity.

I’ve already gotten my gear on (overalls and boots over my own clothes) to work with the cows. Each one has a name and I think Louise said there are about 100+, so I don’t think I’m about to learn all of their names at once.

Marcus has two beautiful dogs which are both quite friendly, and there are birds all over the barn.

Overall the farm is very peaceful and spacious. I am quite excited to spend time here.
